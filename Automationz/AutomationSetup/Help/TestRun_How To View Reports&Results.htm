How To View Test Run Results & Reports?

1. Login as User

2. Under 'Testing' Click on 'Results & Execution'

3. Click on 'Run ID' to see Results & Report

4. Pick a Test Case & Click on 'View' to look at Test Steps

5. Click on 'Detail' to view details of Test Run

6. Click on 'Report' to show report

7. Click on 'Graph' to show report pie chart

8. Choose 'Email' and click on 'Send Reports' to send copy of Report
